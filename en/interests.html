Scientific Interests

  • System analysis (control theory, theory of discrete event systems, desynchronized systems, mathematical modeling)
  • Nonlinear analysis and its applications
  • Linear functional analysis
  • Numerical methods
  • Qualitative problems of differential equations and operator equations
  • Bifurcation theory
  • Asynchronous neural networks
  • Influence of temporal/spatial discretization on behavior of continuous dynamical systems
  • Investigation of the generalized spectral radius for a set of linear operators
